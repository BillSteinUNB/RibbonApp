## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Ribbon. This Privacy Policy explains how Bill Stein ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use the Ribbon mobile application (the "App").

By using Ribbon, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account and use the App:

- **Account Information:** Email address, password (encrypted), user ID, account creation date
- **Profile Information:** Name, avatar/profile picture, user preferences
- **Subscription Information:** Premium subscription status, trial usage count, subscription dates

### 2.2 Recipient and Gift Data

To provide personalized gift recommendations, we collect information about gift recipients you add to the App:

- **Recipient Details:** Name, relationship type (e.g., spouse, parent, friend)
- **Demographic Information:** Age range, gender preference
- **Preferences:** Interests, dislikes, hobbies
- **Budget Information:** Minimum and maximum gift budget, currency preference
- **Occasion Details:** Type of occasion (birthday, holiday, anniversary, etc.), date
- **Gift History:** Past gifts given, gift descriptions, prices, purchase dates, stores, URLs
- **Notes:** Any custom notes you add about recipients

### 2.3 Device and Technical Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, device ID
- **Biometric Capability:** Detection of whether your device supports Face ID, Touch ID, or other biometric authentication (we do NOT collect or store actual biometric data)
- **Push Notification Tokens:** Device tokens for sending push notifications
- **App Usage Data:** App version, feature usage, screen views, interaction timestamps

### 2.4 Analytics and Usage Data

We collect analytics data to improve the App:

- App lifecycle events (app starts, backgrounding)
- Screen views and navigation patterns
- Authentication events (sign up, sign in, sign out)
- Recipient management events (create, update, delete)
- Gift generation events (start, completion, failures)
- Gift interactions (save, unsave, mark as purchased)
- Subscription events (view, start, complete, cancel)
- Upgrade prompt interactions

### 2.5 Communication Preferences

- Email notification preferences
- Push notification preferences
- Marketing communication preferences
- Occasion reminder settings
- Weekly digest preferences

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Provide Services:** To operate the App, authenticate users, and deliver gift recommendations
- **AI Gift Generation:** To send recipient information to our AI service (MiniMax M2.1) to generate personalized gift ideas
- **Notifications:** To send occasion reminders and weekly digests about upcoming events
- **Account Management:** To manage your account, subscriptions, and trial periods
- **Service Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve app features
- **Security:** To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
- **Communication:** To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

### 4.1 Supabase (Database and Authentication)

- **Purpose:** User authentication, database storage, and account management
- **Data Shared:** Email, encrypted password, user profile, recipient data, gift history, subscription information
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

### 4.2 MiniMax M2.1 AI API

- **Purpose:** AI-powered gift recommendation generation
- **Data Shared:** Recipient information (interests, age range, gender, occasion details, budget) to generate gift suggestions
- **Important:** We send recipient data to MiniMax AI to process gift recommendations. This data is used solely for generating suggestions and is not used for training AI models.
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://www.minimaxi.com/privacy-policy](https://www.minimaxi.com/privacy-policy)

### 4.3 Expo (Push Notifications)

- **Purpose:** Delivery of push notifications
- **Data Shared:** Device push tokens, notification content
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** With third-party services listed in Section 4 to operate the App
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Safety and Security:** To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly consent to sharing your information

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after account deletion
- **Recipient and Gift Data:** Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS and at rest in our database
- **Authentication:** Secure password hashing and optional biometric authentication
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Secure Storage:** Data stored using industry-standard security practices via Supabase
- **Regular Updates:** Security measures are regularly reviewed and updated

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Biometric Authentication

**Important Clarification:**

- The App supports biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) as a convenience feature
- **We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any biometric data**
- Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system
- We only detect whether your device supports biometric authentication and store your preference to enable/disable it
- Your actual biometric data (facial features, fingerprints) never leaves your device

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information should contact us immediately.
